Destinations That We offer To Our Members


Hampi, Karnataka

  • Summary:Explore the ancient ruins, climb boulders, and relax by the Tungabhadra River.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Explore the Virupaksha Temple complex, climb Matanga Hill for panoramic views, and visit the Lotus Mahal.
    • Day 2: Hike to the Vithala Temple, explore the Elephant Stables, and visit the Hazara Rama Temple.
    • Day 3: Relax by the Tungabhadra River, visit the Zenana Enclosure, and enjoy a sunset view from Virupaksha Temple.


    • Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir

  • Summary:Trek to Pangong Lake, visit monasteries, and experience the beauty of the Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost:₹15,000-20,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Drive to Pangong Lake, enjoy the changing colors of the lake, and camp overnight.
    • Day 2: Explore the nearby villages, visit the Thiksey Monastery, and enjoy the panoramic views of the Himalayas.
    • Day 3: Drive back to Leh, visit the Leh Palace, and explore the markets.


    • Andaman and Nicobar Islands

  • Summary:Snorkel or scuba dive in crystal-clear waters, explore the Cellular Jail, and relax on pristine beaches.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹15,000-20,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Explore Cellular Jail, visit the Ross Island, and enjoy a sunset cruise.
    • Day 2: Go snorkeling or scuba diving at Radhanagar Beach, visit the Baratang Island, and enjoy a boat ride through the mangroves.
    • Day 3: Relax on the beaches of Havelock Island, enjoy water sports, and explore the natural beauty of the islands.


    • Goa

  • Summary:Party on Anjuna Beach, explore Old Goa's churches, and relax on the beaches of South Goa.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Party on Anjuna Beach, explore the Old Goa churches, and enjoy a sunset cruise.
    • Day 2: Visit the Dudhsagar Falls, explore the spice plantations, and relax on the beaches of South Goa.
    • Day 3: Explore the flea market at Anjuna Beach, visit the Aguada Fort, and enjoy the nightlife in Baga.


    • Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

  • Summary:Practice yoga and meditation, white-water raft, and explore the beautiful Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Practice yoga and meditation, explore the ashrams, and enjoy the Ganga Aarti.
    • Day 2: Go white-water rafting, visit the Lakshman Jhula, and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the Himalayas.
    • Day 3: Visit the Triveni Ghat, explore the local markets, and relax by the Ganga River.


    • Kerala

  • Summary: Explore the backwaters, visit tea plantations, and relax on the beaches of Kovalam or Varkala.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹15,000-20,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Explore the backwaters of Alleppey, enjoy a houseboat cruise, and visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary.
    • Day 2: Visit the Periyar National Park, enjoy a boat safari, and explore the spice plantations.
    • Day 3: Relax on the beaches of Kovalam or Varkala, enjoy Ayurvedic massage, and explore the local markets.


    • Manali, Himachal Pradesh

  • Summary: Trek to Rohtang Pass, explore the Solang Valley, and experience the beauty of the Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Visit the Hadimba Temple, explore the Solang Valley, and enjoy adventure activities like paragliding or skiing.
    • Day 2: Trek to Rohtang Pass, enjoy the panoramic views of the Himalayas, and explore the Beas River.
    • Day 3: Visit the Jogini Falls, explore the local markets, and enjoy the nightlife in Manali.


    • Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

  • Summary: Explore the high-altitude desert, visit monasteries, and experience the tranquility of the Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹15,000-20,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Drive to Kaza, explore the Tabo Monastery, and enjoy the panoramic views of the Himalayas.
    • Day 2: Visit the Kibber Monastery, trek to the Chandratal Lake, and enjoy the beauty of the high-altitude desert.
    • Day 3: Drive back to Kaza, visit the Key Monastery, and explore the local markets.


    • Zanskar Valley, Jammu and Kashmir

  • Summary: Trek to Chadar Lake, experience the frozen river, and explore the remote beauty of the Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹20,000-25,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Trek to Chadar Lake, experience the frozen river, and camp overnight.
    • Day 2: Explore the Zanskar Valley, visit the Phugtal Monastery, and enjoy the stunning scenery.
    • Day 3: Trek back to Leh, visit the Leh Palace, and explore the local markets.


    • Darjeeling, West Bengal

  • Summary: Visit tea plantations, ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, and enjoy stunning views of the Himalayas.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, visit the Tiger Hill for sunrise, and explore the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute.
    • Day 2: Visit the Tea Gardens, explore the Happy Valley, and enjoy the panoramic views of the Himalayas.
    • Day 3: Visit the Ghoom Monastery, explore the local markets, and enjoy the nightlife in Darjeeling.


    • Shillong, Meghalaya

  • Summary: Explore the Cherrapunji waterfalls, visit the Umiam Lake, and experience the beauty of the Northeast.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Visit the Umiam Lake, explore the Elephant Falls, and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
    • Day 2: Trek to the Cherrapunji waterfalls, explore the Mawlynnong Village, and enjoy the local cuisine.
    • Day 3: Visit the Shillong Peak, explore the local markets, and enjoy the nightlife in Shillong.


    • Kutch, Gujarat

  • Summary: Explore the white salt desert, visit Rann of Kutch, and experience the vibrant culture of Gujarat.
  • Approximate Cost: ₹10,000-15,000 (includes accommodation, transportation, food, and activities).
    • Day 1: Explore the Rann of Kutch, enjoy the sunset view, and visit the Kala Dungar.
    • Day 2: Visit the Vijay Vilas Palace, explore the local villages, and enjoy the vibrant culture of Gujarat.
    • Day 3: Visit the Mandvi Beach, explore the Mandvi Palace, and enjoy the local cuisine.
