Customize Your Trip


Customize your holiday with US!!


Have you and your squad been waiting to explore your dream destination? We think it’s time!


Now that you have decided to take the plunge, let’s get cracking. The foundation to an amazing trip is the planning that goes into it before you step on the plane. This is where we, the travel experts come in. With our in-depth knowledge of the countries you plan to visit, we will assist in curating an unforgettable journey at a pace that suits your wish list and your budget.

After watching movies such as Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara and Eat, Pray, Love, we are sure you are longing to take that Euro trip with your friends, your significant other or ride solo! From the architecture in Spain, lip smacking food in Italy, clear blue waters of Croatia to the “coffee shops” in Amsterdam and insane nightlife in Berlin, Europe has something for all kinds of travelers – the museum geek, the adventure junkie and everything in between.


Always wanted to head to Japan and South Korea with your gang but the “language barrier” held you back? Worry not! We have you covered! We can customize your trip to give you an inside peek into the amazing experiences there. Try karaoke, slip into cosplay costumes, enjoy interesting Japanese delicacies, shop for Japanese quirky fashion in hidden streets, stroll through the streets of Gion to get the authentic feel of the Geisha culture, try some cool moves in the Ninja cafe and have many more adventures in Japan. In South Korea you can start with the Korean barbecue, get an insight into the crazy K-pop culture, maybe learn to play a Korean musical instrument, wear a traditional Hanbok, try local soju to quench your ‘thirst’ and have other such fun experiences which will give you a bunch of insta worthy memories!

Curious to explore the biggest country in the world? Russia is one of our favourite places and we can plan a super exciting trip for you! Go 65 metres underground (18 floors) and check out a top secret Bunker 42, go into the most beautiful and deepest metros underground, be awed by the opulent palaces, have a chance to make your own Matrioshka doll, stroll through local Russian markets and discover the land from a local point of view. Fancy taking a ride across in the Trans-Siberian? Get in touch with us and we will be happy to help you unfold Russia!!


Imagine this – you’re floating in freezing water surrounded by the magical snow covered landscape and the Northern Lights appear above!! Lapland is a beautiful mystery which is a delight to uncover and we are sure that chasing the Northern Lights is high up on your bucket list!! Riding snowmobiles, playing with the husky dogs, spending the night in a glass igloo, experiencing Sami hospitality, having a meal in a traditional Kota tent…. We got you covered for it all.

Have you always been intrigued by the mystic of the Middle East? Well, Turkey and Israel are right up our alley! They are perfect destinations for the girls trip you have forever been planning! The traditional souks in Istanbul, fairy chimneys of Cappadocia, hot air ballooning in the early hours…. versus the beaches in Tel Aviv and of course the absolutely delicious hummus and baklava that cannot be missed. The ancient streets of these cities carry stories of a thousand years and come dusk, the whole energy changes and gives way to a vibrant nightlife.


Get in touch with us to plan your dreamy and fun getaway to these exotic destinations.
